Jjajangmyeon

Description

Jjajangmyeon is a Korean/Chinese noodle dish made with black bean paste, vegetables, and beef.

Ingredients

  • Jjajangmyeon Noodles
  • Pork Belly
  • Korean Radish
  • Zucchini
  • Potatoe
  • Onion
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Black Bean Paste
  • Potato Starch Powder
  • Sesame Oil
  • Cucumber

Step by Step

  1. Stir fry pork belly with 1 tablespoon vegetable oil for 4-5 minutes
  2. Add radish and stir fry for 1 minute
  3. Add potato, onion, and Zucchini, and stir fry for 3 minutes
  4. Clear space in center and add 2 tablespoons vegetable oil to center, along with 1/4 cup black bean paste. Stir for 1 minute, then mix everything in
  5. Add 2 cups of water and close lid to cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add sesame oil and remove from heat
  7. Serve with the noodles
