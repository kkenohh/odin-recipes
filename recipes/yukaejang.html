Yukaejang

Description

Yukaejang is a spicy beef Korean soup made with shredded beef and vegetables. Some kinds have noodles called Dangmyeon, while others just have the beef and veggies. You can also eat it by mixing rice with it.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Scallions
  • Daepa (green onions)
  • Gosari (fernbrake fiddleheads)
  • Gochugaru (Korean red chili pepper flakes)
  • *Optional* Dangmyeon (sweet potato starch noodles)
  • *Optional* Eggs
