Yukaejang

Description

Yukaejang is a spicy beef Korean soup made with shredded beef and vegetables. Some kinds have noodles called Dangmyeon, while others just have the beef and veggies. You can also eat it by mixing rice with it.

Ingredients

  • Instant Pot
  • Beef
  • Scallions
  • Onions
  • Sesame Oil
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Gosari (fernbrake fiddleheads)
  • Gochugaru (Korean red chili pepper flakes)
  • *Optional* Dangmyeon (sweet potato starch noodles)
  • *Optional* Eggs

Step by Step

  1. PREPARING GOSARI: Put gosari and 4 cups of water to a small pot. Let it boil while covered until it's tender. Let it cool. Rinse, drain, and cut into 4-inch lengths
  2. PREPARING BROTH AND MEAT: Boil meat and vegetables in 14 cups of water, covered, until the meat is tender. Let meat cool and discard vegetables
  3. Shred the meat to 4-inch strips
  4. Blanch bean sprouts in boiling water for one minute, wash with cold water, and drain. Cut scallions into 4-inch lengths
  5. Heat sesame oil in a pan while stirring in Gochugaru. Turn heat off when oil becomes red and the flakes are pasty
  6. Add meat and gosari to the pan, along with 1 tablespoon soup soy sauce and garlic.Make sure to toss well to coat everything well
  7. Add meat and gosari mixture to the broth. Add 1 tablespoon soup soy sauce and boil while covered for about 10 minutes
  8. Add other vegetables and boil for another 10 minutes. Add salt and pepper until satisfaction
  9. Add noodles and eggs, and turn the heat off
