Donkatsu

Description

Donkatsu is a Japanese dish made from pork, based on the other Japanese dish, katsu.

Ingredients

  • Pork Loin Cutlets
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Cooking Oil
  • *Special Sauce* Ketchup and Mayo
  • Rice
  • Cabbage

Step by Step

  1. Pound cutlets until they are 1/2-inch or less
  2. Sprinkle them with salt and pepper, and dip them in flour
  3. Beat the eggs and dip the cutlets into them
  4. Press cutlets in the bread crumbs so both sides are evenly coated
  5. Heat oil in a skillet to 340 F
  6. Cook one piece of pork at a type in the oil for 3-4 minutes. Then, flip it to cook other side for 2-3 minutes. Let it cool on a wire rack
  7. Repeat Step 6 for each cutlet
  8. Serve with side of shredded cabbage and rice
